TYPE OF QUIZZES

Graded

The quiz has at least one graded question (a question that has a right/wrong answer). It's not a graded quiz.

Weighted

The quiz has weighted questions (different points for each choice). It's not a weighted quiz.

Personality

It is a buzzfeed-style personality quiz. It's not a personality quiz.
